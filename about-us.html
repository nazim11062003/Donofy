About Us

Donatic is a decentralized platform for NGO’s that will help NGO’s grow and also reduce tax evasion. Donofy will take donations in the form of “Ethereum” and divide it equally and donate it to every NGO’S who will be registered on our platform.

For any NGO’s who are willing to register there NGO on our platform, they will have to authorize their NGO from the government first, then only they can register their NGO on our platform.

Donofy offers another feature(i.e.Multisend) that allows a person to do multiple “Ethereum” transactions at a time,which will reduce transaction fees and will also take less time in doing multiple transactions.

It is a convenient solution that will not only help the NGO’s but will also help those who are willing to donate the money to NGO’s but because of trust issues they are not able to donate.

How To Use?

Step 1: Connect your “Meta-Mask” Wallet.

Step 2: Enter the receiver’s address and amount of “Ethereum” to be send.

Step 3: Click on “ADD”.

Step 4: Click on “SEND”.
